Infantry

The Infantry Robot is the most mobile ground robotic platform in RoboMaster. It is equipped with a 17 mm projectile launcher capped at a 20 m/s launch speed. The infantry serves as force multiplier for the Hero Robot taking both a scouting and secondary offensive line role for the team. The infantry is meant for fast and aggressive play, rushing or flanking opponents.

Our 2021-2022 generation is a completely new design compared to previous years. Our emphasis with this design is to solve problems that were prevalent in the RoboMaster competition. This meant overhauling the suspension system and the turret. We are upgrading the Infantry with a suite of new systems including a new computer vision system, super capacitor circuit, power management and the addition of an on board Nvidia Jetson TX2 to provide the required computational power. Our biggest challenge is optimizing our computer vision algorithms to boost analysis frame rate in order to be effective in close quarter situations. This design is still currently under development.
